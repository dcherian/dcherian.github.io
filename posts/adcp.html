Finding ADCP data

NCEI

https://www.nodc.noaa.gov/gocd/jasadcp/index.html Full inventory with high-resolution (short.nc, _long.nc) and hourly/10m averaged data (v3.nc): https://www.nodc.noaa.gov/gocd/jasadcp/hr_access.html

This document is extremely useful at understanding the different file types available at NCEI: https://www.nodc.noaa.gov/gocd/jasadcp/GEN_DOC/Directory_ReadMe_uniqueSonarCruise_SACID.pdf
